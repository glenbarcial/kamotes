Wines



The wines served at Kamotes are imported from artisan wine-growers near and far; women and men with a passion for their craft, a deep respect for their land and a thoughtful approach to their processes.

The list offers wines of distinction, often produced with minimal intervention and always reflective of the place they were grown and the people who have made them.
